- Prep time: ~5 min
- Cook time: ~20-30 min
- Servings: 4

# Ingredients

### Groceries

- Browned butter (Optional)
- Butter
- Floury Potatoes
- Garlic
- Heavy cream
- Olive Oil
- Parmesan (Optional)
- Pepper (white or black)
- Salt
- Thyme

# Directions

### Mise en Place

Ingredients:

- 5 potatoes

1. Peel and dice

### Cooking

Ingredients:

- Butter
- Crushed garlic
- Heavy cream
- Olive oil
- Parmesan (Optional)
- Peeled and diced potatoes
- Salt
- Thyme

1. Place **potatoes**, **thyme**, and **garlic** in a pot with water 
2. Cook for 10-12 min
3. Optional: make **browned butter**
4. Pour out the water, remove the **garlic** and **thyme**
5. Add **butter** and **heavy cream**, mix, repeat until texture is right
6. Add seasoning to taste

- 8 dl milk
- browned butter

- sugar (small amount)

### Mise en Place

#### Pancake

Ingredients:

- salt

- 1 tsp baking powder
- 8 dl milk
- 5 eggs
- browned butter

1. Make browned butter
2. Mix the dry ingredients
3. Mix in the milk, then the eggs, lastly the browned butter
4. Use an electrical mixer to fluff the batter

#### Pork

Ingredients:
- 1 packet of pork

1. Cut into pieces

### Cooking

#### Pork

Ingredients:
- Sliced pork

1. Fry the pork in the roasting pan

#### Pancake

Ingredients:
- Batter
- Fried pork

1. Add the batter to the roasting pan
2. Put in the oven for 20-30 min
